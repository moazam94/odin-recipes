Baked Cherry Pie

Description

After a decadent meal one cannot be satisfied without ending the night with a delicious dessert. This mouth watering, freshly baked, indredibly tasty cherry pie will knock your taste buds out of the park! So dive in and learn how to bake this fun and easy recipe for your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 4 tblsp quick-cooking tapioca
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 4 cups pitted cherries
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
