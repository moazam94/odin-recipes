Freshly Baked Cherry Pie!

Description

After a decadent meal one cannot be satisfied without ending the night with a delicious dessert. This mouth watering, freshly baked, indredibly tasty cherry pie will knock your taste buds out of the park! So dive in and learn how to bake this fun and easy recipe for your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 4 tblsp quick-cooking tapioca
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 4 cups pitted cherries
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Set bottom crust in pie pan. Set top crust aside, covered.
  2. In a large mixing bowl combine tapioca, salt, sugar, cherries, and extracts. Let stand for 15 minutes. Turn out into bottom crust and dot with butter. Cover with top crust, flute edges and cut vents in top. Place pie on a foil-lined cookie sheet--in case of drips.
  3. Bake for 50 minutes in the preaheated oven, unitl golden brown. Let cool for several hours before slicing and Bon Appetit!

Nutrition Facts (per serving)
