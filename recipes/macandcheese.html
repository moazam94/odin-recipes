Homemade Mac and Cheese!

Description

Skip the boxed stuff and make homemade mac and cheese tonight. This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting and will become a staple in your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup salted butter
  • 3 tblsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk, or more as needed
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tblsp salted butter
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch ground paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
  2. Make the macaroni and cheese: Bring large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and simmer, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite for about 8 minutes. It will finish cooking in the oven.
  3. Drain and transfer to the prepared baking dish.
  4. While the macaroni is cooking, melt 1/4 cup butter in medium skillet over low heat. Whisk in flour and stir until the mixture becomes paste-like and light golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Gradually whisk 2 1/2 cups milk into the flour mixture, and bring to a simmer. Stir in shredded Cheddar and finely grated Parmesan cheeses; season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce has thickened, 3 to 5 minutes, adding up to 1/2 cup more milk if needed. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir until well combined.
  6. Make the bread crumb topping: Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add bread crumbs: cook and stir until well coated and browned. Spread bread crumbs over macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  7. Bake in the preaheated oven until toppping is golden brown and macaroni and cheese is bubbling, about 30 minutes and VOILA!
